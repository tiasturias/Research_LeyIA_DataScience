[9 December 2025] Brookfield and Qai form $20 billion strategic investment partnership for AI infrastructure:
Brookfield and Qai, a subsidiary of the Qatar Investment Authority, have formed a $20 billion joint venture to develop AI infrastructure in Qatar and select international markets. This partnership aims to position Qatar as a leading AI hub in the Middle East by building fully integrated AI facilities, including an Integrated Compute center to expand regional access to high-performance computing. The initiative aligns with Qatar's National Vision 2030, focusing on economic diversification and innovation. Brookfield plans to invest through its Artificial Intelligence Infrastructure Fund, which seeks to mobilize up to $100 billion globally.

[2 February 2025] Arab League calls for AI regulation framework:
It is reported that at the Arab Dialogue Circle on Artificial Intelligence in the Arab World, Arab League Secretary General Ahmad Abu Al-Gheit has urged scholars to draft a regulatory framework for AI that aligns with Arab values and interests. The call to action includes (1) addressing the rapid developments in AI, particularly in large language models and generative AI, (2) localizing AI industries and investing in strategic planning and financial resources, and (3) ensuring active participation in the global AI race to achieve safe and culturally aligned AI integration.

[5 December 2024] UK and Qatar launch project to boost artificial intelligence collaboration:
The UK and Qatar have launched a joint AI research commission, seeking to establish a roadmap for UK-Qatar collaboration on AI in a range of areas across the spectrum of ecosystem development, policy and regulation, security, and international engagement. The joint study will be led by Queen Mary University of London, in partnership with Hamad bin Khalifa University (HBKU) in Qatar.
